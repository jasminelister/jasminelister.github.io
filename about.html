About Me

Teaching Background and Approach

I’m currently in my third year at Australian Catholic University (ACU), working toward a double degree being a Bachelor of Secondary Education alongside a Bachelor of Arts in Design Technologies and Innovations. My major focus is Industrial Technology (timber and metal), and I’ve complemented that with a minor in Food Technologies. Over the course of my studies, I’ve completed three teaching practicum placements that have been instrumental in shaping my practical skills in real classroom environments: first at St George Christian School in Hurstville, next at St John Bosco College in Engadine, and most recently at Lucas Heights Community School

About Me

My Journey as a Pre-Service Teacher

I’m currently in my third year at Australian Catholic University (ACU), working toward a double degree: Bachelor of Secondary Education alongside a Bachelor of Arts (Design Technologies and Innovations).

My major focus is Industrial Technology (timber and metal), and I’ve complemented that with a minor in Food Technologies. Throughout my studies, I’ve completed three teaching practicum placements that have been instrumental in shaping my skills within real classroom environments:

  • St George Christian School — Hurstville
  • St John Bosco College — Engadine
  • Lucas Heights Community School — Barden Ridge

My Teaching Experience

Throughout the duration of my three practicums I have taught both Timber and Food Technology. After the completion of my studies, these will continue to be my main teaching areas.

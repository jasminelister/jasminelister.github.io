Stage 4–6 & University Course Projects

A selection of practical design and teaching projects completed during my degree.

Stage 4 Timber Project — Phone Box

A phone box constructed using hand tools and incorporating laser-cut plastic designs for decorative engraving. This project introduced foundational joinery and safe workshop practices.

Stage 5 Timber Project — Heirloom Box

A refined storage box using mitred corner joints and veneer patterning techniques. This design can be adapted for Year 10 students to extend their fine woodworking skills.

University Timber Lamp Design

A unique lamp designed and manufactured as part of a university design technologies unit, focusing on aesthetic balance, safe electrical integration, and precise joinery.
